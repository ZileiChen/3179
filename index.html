Ore Mining in Australia

Ore mining is a critical component of the nations GDP. Mining in Australia amounts to roughly 75% of the country's exports.

*Note: various charts can use legends as filters

Mining is the biggest industry in Australia. The top 3 exports as well as production in terms of monetary value is iron, coal and gold.

However, gold is one of the least mined materials in Australia. This is due to the price of gold, being valued a lot higher compared to iron and coal, thus less gold is required to be mined for more profit.

Western Australia mines the most ores and minerals out of all other states, this is due to the high number of mines they have compared to other states. Majority of Western Autralia's mining profits also came from the last 40 years, where a massive mining boom occured in Australia

As you can see, majority of our profits still come from iron and coal, making up almost 70 percent of the mining industry's export profits in the year 2019.
