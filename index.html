Ore Mining in Australia

Ore mining is a critical component of the nations GDP. Mining in Australia amou nts to roughly 75% of the country's exports.

Mining is the biggest industry in Australia. The top 3 exports as well as production in terms of value is iron, coal and gold. However, gold is one of the least mined materials in Australia. This is due to the price of gold, being valued a lot higher compared to iron and coal, thus less gold is required to be mined for more profit.
